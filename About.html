Creative Stop Shop

Created by Adina and Sheri, Creative Stop Shop endeavours to be a one stop shop for all your creative needs.

When it comes to putting together an event, we have made the process a lot simpler and faster so that you are able to find the best talent in the city in one click. We work with the most talented and highly sought creatives in the industry.

Creative Stop Shop helps you to filter searches according to your needs, the website will produce a personalised selection of creatives needed for your events and their availability. In addition to this, the website takes into account your budget for your events, projects, shoots and projects.

Code First Girls


Creative Stop Shop is sponsored by Code First: Girls. Code First: Girls is a Not for Profit Social Enterprise that trains women in IT skills and helps companies to develop more female friendly recruitment policies.
The organisation promotes gender diversity and female participation in the technology sector by offering free and paid training and courses for students and professional women.



About

The Creative Stop Shop is a new startup powered by GSquared founders; Sheri and Adina. The Creative Stop Shop aims to fulfill all of your creative needs. We work with the most talented creatives around the uk who are available to book via our website to help you reach your creative goals.
